Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to the recommended temperature for your pizza dough.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread marinara sauce evenly over the pizza dough. Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  4. Top with pepperoni, bell peppers, black olives, and red onions. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with dried oregano and garlic powder. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven according to the pizza dough instructions or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
